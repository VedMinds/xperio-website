Account Deletion

Overview

At Xperio, we respect your right to control your data. This page explains how to delete your account and associated information from our platform.

What Gets Deleted

Account deletion will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information
  • Booking history
  • Saved preferences
  • Reviews and ratings

Deletion Process

  1. Send an account deletion request to info@xperio.travel from your registered email address
  2. Include your full name and registered phone number for verification
  3. We'll verify your identity and send a confirmation email
  4. Your account will be deleted within 48 hours of confirmation

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Any active bookings must be completed or cancelled before deletion
  • Certain information may be retained for legal compliance

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion, please contact us at info@xperio.travel
