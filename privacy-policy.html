Privacy Policy

Welcome to Xperio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you:

What personal information do we process?

When you visit, use, or navigate our Services, we may process personal information including:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Booking details and preferences
  • Payment information
  • Usage data and interactions with our platform

How do we use your information?

We use your information to:

  • Facilitate bookings and experiences
  • Improve our services

  • Ensure platform security

How do we keep your information safe?

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to protecting your data using best practices.
