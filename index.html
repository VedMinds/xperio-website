About Us

Xperio is a digital platform that connects travelers directly with local hosts for authentic, cultural, and immersive experiences. The tech platform facilitates discovery, booking, and financial transactions, ensuring a seamless connection between the traveler's desire for unique experiences and the host's offer of local authenticity. All these experiences will be delivered through sustainable practices.

Xperio addresses the growing demand for authentic and immersive travel experiences that go beyond conventional sightseeing tours.

Authenticity First: Focus on deep connection and immersion, rather than standard tourist offerings.
Local Economic Empowerment: A business model that prioritizes local hosts, ensuring they receive a fair share of the revenue.
Sustainability: Encouraging experiences that promote environmental sustainability and responsible tourism.
